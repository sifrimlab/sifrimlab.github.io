This week, the LMIB team held its annual lab retreat in Gulpen, nestled in the beautiful Limburg countryside, an ideal setting to rest, recharge, reflect on the past year, and plan our next steps as a lab.

During the retreat, we explored Maastricht and its rich history, put our teamwork to the test in an escape room, and took part in workshops on collaboration, communication, and the effective use of AI tools.
